Alfredo Recipe

The Worlds Best Alfredo

Lirum larum Alfredo. Best ever. Only takes 10min of cooking and costs a whooping 5$

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season, and stir in the cheese.
  3. Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce.
